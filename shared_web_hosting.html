    Fed up of hosts who have UK websites but after you’ve signed up, you’re assigned a datacentre across the pond in the US? With Jolt, you’ll only ever be hosted in the UK, at our secure facility in the Midlands.

    Going the extra mile above and beyond our competitors, we deliver a better overall hosting experience. Don’t believe us? In the very unlikely event that you are not satisfied with our hosting services, we offer a full 45-day money back guarantee for peace of mind.

    Cluttered, hard-to-use interfaces? Outdated tools? Complicated pricing plans? Not with Jolt. We use the industry-standard cPanel control panel, which sits at the core of your hosting.     What’s rock-solid hosting without a rockstar support team? We’ve got all bases covered with a technical support team working 24/7/365 to help you with any questions or issues.
