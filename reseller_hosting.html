How does it work?

Reseller Hosting Explained

4 Core Components: WebHostManager, cPanel, WHMCS and Jolt’s amazing support. These 4 components come together to empower you as a reseller / webmaster operating multiple websites.

WebHostManager – the reseller control centre.

From here, you create, manage and maintain all of your resold / sub-accounts. You can define packages, view stats and much more all from within your browser.

cPanel – everyone’s favourite control panel.

Each account you create in WebHostManager (WHM for short) comes with its own cPanel login. From inside cPanel, you can quickly set up email, install WordPress in a couple of clicks, access files/databases and much more. For example, this means you can create multiple WordPress websites in just a couple of clicks – a true agency favourite.

WHMCS – the billing engine for WebHostManager.

If you need to bill your clients for hosting services, domains or other components, WHMCS makes it easy. WHMCS also includes an easy to use helpdesk/ticket system. WHMCS and cPanel can be integrated with a Single Sign-On option.

Jolt’s Support – the fastest, friendliest and easiest to use around.

We’re on Live Chat 24 hours a day. We’ll answer any question, big or small and we’re your partners to make reseller hosting a success.

Setup is instant and you can begin using reseller hosting in under 5 minutes.
