Dedicated Server Features

Jolt’s dedicated servers only use Dell and Supermicro Enterprise hardware. Hardware is new, bare metal (no virtualisation), uses enterprise grade components and is hosted from our UK datacentres. Our Dedicated Servers include a generous bandwidth allowance, DDoS protection, remote management features and a range of software and management choices. Servers are setup by default in our Nottingham datacentre with London available by request.

  • Same-Day Server Setup

    From the moment you place your order, our technical team will begin provisioning your server hardware. We’ll then begin the process of installing your Operating System, configuring add-ons such as cPanel/WHM, and securing your server. We aim to provide a fully configured server within 4 hours.

  • 24/7/365 Customer Support

    Our experienced technical team are available to assist with any issues you may have 24/7, 365 days a year. Choose a managed server to get fast, friendly support around the clock. We’re your partner in helping your business grow

  • 100% Network Uptime Guarantee

    We offer a rock solid 100% network uptime SLA across all of our servers. We operate a fast, resilient and reliable UK network connecting your server to the outside world.
